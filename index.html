About The Course

The Faculty of Nursing at Sigma University, with over 18 years of excellence, offers a Government, GNC, and INC-approved Nursing program. Combining strong academics with hands-on clinical training, it features modern infrastructure and expert faculty. With a limited intake of 40 students, the program ensures personalized learning and prepares graduates for rewarding careers in leading healthcare institutions.

  • 18+ years of excellence, GNC & INC approved.
  • Strong academics with practical training.
  • Scholarships & affordable fees.
  • Career-ready graduates for top healthcare roles.

Our Specialized Nursing Programs

A foundation course focused on basic patient care, midwifery, and community health. **Duration: 2 Years.**

Key Modules: Community Health Nursing, Health Promotion, Midwifery Practice.

A comprehensive program covering general nursing skills for clinical and community settings. **Duration: 3 Years.**

Key Modules: Medical-Surgical Nursing, Pediatrics, Mental Health Nursing.

The standard undergraduate degree, preparing students for professional licensure and advanced clinical roles. **Duration: 4 Years.**

Key Modules: Nursing Research, Pharmacology, Critical Care Nursing, Management of Nursing Services.

Specialization Areas: Cardiac Nursing, Emergency Triage, Ventilator Management.

Key Modules: Neonatal Care, Child Psychology, Pediatric Pharmacology.
